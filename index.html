Ways to contact Council

Phone

Contact Council 24 hours a day, 7 days a week

07 3403 8888

Phone us for urgent issues such as:

  • lost animals
  • lost property
  • dangerous dogs
  • traffic light problems
  • public safety concerns

We aim to answer your call within 20 seconds.

Customer Centres

Talk to Council in person at Council's Customer Centres for:

  • rates enquiries
  • animal registration
  • local laws and compliance
  • licensing, permits and applications
  • account payments

Email

You can email Council using the online form.

If you request a Council service, we'll provide you a reference number within 3-5 business days.

SMS

Report problems using your mobile device to:

0429 2 FIX IT (0429 234 948)

Take a photo and message details including the location and your name.
